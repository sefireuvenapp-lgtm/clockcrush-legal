Privacy Policy

Effective date / Last updated: 03/07/2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ClockCrush ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our application. We built ClockCrush to work without accounts, servers, or personal data collection — this policy explains exactly what that means in practice, and what our third-party ad provider collects on our behalf.

1. Information We Collect

ClockCrush does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to us. We have no backend servers, no user accounts, and no analytics tools of our own.

The only information the App stores is saved locally on your device and never leaves it. This includes:

  • Your current countdown timer state
  • Your mute/sound preference
  • Your selected language
  • Your earned stars/milestone progress

This local data is not accessible to us and is deleted automatically if you uninstall the App.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

ClockCrush displays banner ads and rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. To deliver and measure ads, AdMob may automatically collect certain information from your device, such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • IP address / general location (country/region level)
  • App interaction data (e.g., ad impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us, and is subject to Google's own privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use its services here:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

3. Your Choices Regarding Ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

You can also reset your Advertising ID at any time from the same menu.

4. Children's Privacy

ClockCrush is not directed at children and is not designed for use by children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Ads served in the App are not configured under Google's Ads for Families program.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data, because we do not collect any. The only data shared with a third party is the advertising-related data described in Section 2, which is collected directly by Google AdMob under its own privacy policy.

6. Data Security

Since all app data (timer state, preferences, progress) is stored only locally on your device, it is protected by your device's own security features (lock screen, encryption, etc.). We do not transmit this data anywhere.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes in our advertising partners or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
